Indian Culture

Traditions and Customs

Indian culture is known for its rich traditions, including various classical dance forms like Bharatanatyam and Kathak. Indian weddings are elaborate and vibrant, reflecting the diversity and traditions of the country. Festivals like Diwali and Holi are celebrated with great enthusiasm.

Festivals

Key Indian festivals include Diwali, the Festival of Lights, and Holi, the Festival of Colors. Diwali is celebrated with lights, fireworks, and sweets, while Holi involves vibrant colors and joyous celebrations.

Arts and Crafts

Indian arts include traditional crafts such as intricate rangoli designs, pottery, and textile weaving. The art of henna, known for its beautiful patterns, is often used in ceremonies and celebrations.
