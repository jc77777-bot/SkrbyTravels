Malay Culture

Traditions and Customs

The Malay culture is rich with traditions and customs, including traditional dances such as the Joget and Silat martial arts. The cultural heritage is also reflected in Malay architecture, clothing, and ceremonies such as the traditional wedding ceremonies known as "Bersanding."

Festivals

Key festivals in Malay culture include Hari Raya Aidilfitri and Hari Raya Haji. Hari Raya Aidilfitri marks the end of Ramadan and is celebrated with feasts and special prayers. Hari Raya Haji commemorates the sacrifice of Ibrahim and involves rituals of sacrifice and community gatherings.

Arts and Crafts

Malay arts include intricate batik textiles and traditional wood carvings. Batik is known for its beautiful patterns and vibrant colors, while traditional wood carvings often feature intricate designs and motifs that reflect Malay folklore and beliefs.
