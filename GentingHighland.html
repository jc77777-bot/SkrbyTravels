Welcome to Genting Highlands

Genting Highlands is a popular hill resort destination in Malaysia, offering a wide range of attractions including theme parks, casinos, shopping centers, and breathtaking views. Explore some of the top attractions below.

Skyway Cable Car

Experience the thrill of soaring above lush landscapes with the Skyway Cable Car. This iconic ride offers breathtaking views as it takes you high up into the mountains, giving you a panoramic view of the entire Genting Highlands region.

Genting Theme Park

The Genting Theme Park is a must-visit for families and thrill-seekers alike. With a range of exhilarating rides, from roller coasters to water rides, and plenty of activities for all ages, it's the perfect spot for a day of fun and excitement.

Genting Casino

As one of Asia's most famous casinos, the Genting Casino offers a world-class gaming experience. Whether you're a seasoned gambler or just looking for some entertainment, the casino provides a luxurious and exciting atmosphere to test your luck.
