Popular Places

Langkawi is an archipelago made up of 99 islands on Malaysia’s west coast. Surrounded by turquoise sea, the interior of the main island is a mixture of picturesque paddy fields and jungle-clad hills. If you’re intent on carting off duty-free alcohol, cigarettes and chocolate, then this is the place to be. There’s much more to do here than just lazing around on the beach.

Explore Penang

Penang, often known as the Pearl of the Orient, is famous for its soft sandy beaches and is fondly regarded as the food capital of Malaysia. With its enchanting historical buildings and colorful cultural landscape, Penang is a top destination for tourists.
