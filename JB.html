Welcome to Johor Bahru

Johor Bahru, the capital city of Johor in Malaysia, is known for its vibrant attractions and cultural landmarks. Explore some of the top attractions below.

Legoland Malaysia

Legoland Malaysia is a family-friendly theme park with a range of exciting rides and attractions based on the popular LEGO toys. Enjoy a day filled with adventure, creativity, and fun for all ages.

Johor Bahru Old Chinese Temple

The Johor Bahru Old Chinese Temple is a historic temple that showcases the rich cultural heritage of the Chinese community in Johor Bahru. Its traditional architecture and historical significance make it a must-visit landmark.

Putuo Temple

The Putuo Temple in Johor Bahru is a serene and spiritual site that highlights the area's cultural and religious diversity. Its peaceful ambiance and architectural beauty make it a noteworthy destination.
