Welcome to Melaka

Melaka, also known as Malacca, is a historic city in Malaysia renowned for its rich cultural heritage and historical significance. Explore some of the top attractions below.

Melaka River Cruise

Enjoy a relaxing cruise along the Melaka River and take in the picturesque views of the city. The cruise offers a unique perspective of Melaka's historical buildings and vibrant street art.

Stadthuys

The Stadthuys is a historic building located in the heart of Melaka. It was once the residence of the Dutch Governor and his officers. Today, it serves as a museum showcasing Melaka's rich history and culture.

Cheng Hoon Teng Temple

The Cheng Hoon Teng Temple is one of the oldest Chinese temples in Malaysia. It is known for its beautiful architecture and intricate carvings, serving as an important cultural and spiritual site for the local Chinese community.

Jonker Street

Jonker Street is famous for its vibrant night market, where you can find a variety of local delicacies, antiques, and souvenirs. The street is a lively place to experience Melaka's culture and enjoy some shopping and dining.
